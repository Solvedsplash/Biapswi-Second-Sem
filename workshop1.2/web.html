    About Us

    Pagani is a luxury sports car manufacturer founded in 1992 by Horacio Pagani, an Argentinian-Italian engineer and designer. The company is based in Modena, Italy, and is known for its limited-edition hypercars that are renowned for their exquisite design, cutting-edge technology, and unparalleled performance.
